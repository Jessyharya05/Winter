TRCFor Everyone

Happiness With Recycling

Everyone can recycle, and the world would look very different if everyone started recycling. Take your step and stop the greatest ocean pollution in history.

Program Offer

1

Pick Up

Submit your form of your recyclable waste, then specify the pickup time and location. TRcycle partners will confirm the pickup according to the time and location you've set. The partner will weigh and pay for your waste in cash or Points. You will receive an online sales receipt once the transaction is successful. You can also provide ratings and suggestions to our collection partners at the end of each transaction. With TRcycle, recycling becomes easier

2

Drop Off

Directly drop off your recyclable waste at the nearest Recycling Centre. You can recycle starting from a small size, such as a single plastic bottle. Your waste will be weighed, and the partner will pay you in cash or Points. You will receive a sales report after the transaction is successful. You can also provide ratings and suggestions to the collection partners at the end of each transaction. With TRcycle, recycling becomes easier.

Social-Economy Impact That You Will Get

  • 1

    Social and economic impact by empowering local waste collectors while simultaneously increasing the national recycling rate.

  • 2

    Actively involved in efforts to prevent plastic waste leakage into the ocean.

  • 3

    Easy and secure payments, either in cash or through Points.

  • 4

    View your recycling activity reports and make progress every day.
